About Our District

The District Pastor

Pastor Joel Zemba, born in 1997, holds a master's degree in Computer Science and IT and a bachelor's degree in Theology. He is the author of When God Gets Personal and a passionate home chef who blends faith, tech, and flavor in everyday life.

Chalata Mission District, organised in 2021, we have been serving our community with the love of Christ for over 4 years. We believe in the power of the Gospel to transform lives and communities.
